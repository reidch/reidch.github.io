## Research goal

WorldMesh explores how generated 3D environments can preserve explicit semantic structure and become executable inside a physical simulation.

## Pipeline

1. Structured world representation
2. Blender scaffold generation
3. Camera planning
4. Depth-conditioned appearance generation
5. 3D asset replacement
6. Texture fusion and local refinement
7. Physics backend

> The page content, media and links are all independent from the rendering code of this website.
