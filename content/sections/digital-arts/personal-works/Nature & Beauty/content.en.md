# Nature & Beauty

Nature does not exist only in mountains, forests and vast landscapes. It can also be found in changing light, growing plants, flowing water and the small structures that are easily overlooked.

This series explores my observation and imagination of natural beauty through form, color and atmosphere. Rather than simply reproducing real scenery, I try to capture the feelings created by nature and transform them into personal visual worlds through composition, light and color.

## Themes

The series focuses on several interconnected qualities of nature:

- Growth and transformation
- Order and randomness
- Softness and strength
- Transience and permanence
- The relationship between life and environment

## Process

The works develop through observation, photographic references, color studies, compositional sketches and final painting. Some pieces begin with real landscapes, while others reinterpret natural forms through imagination.
